# Creative Strategy Backlog

These ideas are research candidates for later iterations. They are not approved live systems.

The point is to test weird but falsifiable mechanisms while keeping the eventual operating set small.

## Creative Candidate Families

1. Regime-conditioned momentum: use macro and market-state drivers to switch lookback speed.
2. Concentration fuse: permit QQQ/SMH leadership only while breadth and credit remain stable.
3. AI capex boom/bust barbell: pair AI-beta momentum with hard stops from semis breadth and credit.
4. Policy-whipsaw throttle: reduce target size after large headline/event reversals for several sessions.
5. Oil shock playbook: separate energy-led risk-on reflation from energy-led stagflation shock.
6. Liquidity impulse overlay: use Fed balance sheet, TGA, reverse repo, reserves, dollar, and credit state.
7. Earnings-revision surrogate: use sector ETF leadership and mega-cap dispersion until direct revisions data exists.
8. Defensive leadership early warning: treat persistent GLD/TLT/low-vol leadership as equity-risk decay.
9. Breadth repair re-entry: require RSP/SPY and IWM/MGC confirmation before re-risking after drawdown.
10. Volatility crush re-entry: re-enter risk only after VIX/VXN pressure fades and credit confirms.
11. Macro surprise drift: use recent economic releases versus consensus once a calendar data source is available.
12. Slow crash detector: combine rolling lower highs in QQQ/RSP, HYG/LQD, and semis breadth.
13. False breakout penalty: penalize assets that repeatedly fail 1-month highs within a short window.
14. Sector crowding unwind: fade sectors with extreme VAMS but deteriorating breadth/credit confirmation.
15. Dollar wrecking-ball filter: reduce EM, commodities, and cyclicals when dollar pressure rises.
16. Inflation split-state model: distinguish disinflationary growth support from recessionary disinflation.
17. Drawdown memory sizing: position size depends on current drawdown plus time since prior peak.
18. Multi-armed allocation tournament: allocate small research sleeves to top candidates with decay/relegation.
19. Ensemble-as-overlay: combine only the risk signals from several systems, not their full portfolios.
20. Narrative/event shock index: classify news into event families and require market confirmation before action.

## Constraints

- Long-only by default.
- No shorting.
- No derivatives as operating strategies.
- Human-triggered latency.
- Use non-tradable data only as signals, never as assumed execution assets.
- Promote no more than 1-3 systems toward paper/live monitoring.
